About Us

Cloud Shift Inc. is an Ottawa, Ontario-based Canadian company founded in 2019 by Jason Matte and Derrick Poon Young. Together, they bring over 35 years of North American and international business experience in enterprise architecture, business transformation, software development, and digital modernization. Cloud Shift engages with diverse clients in Canada, the United States, and internationally. Its employees and collaborators are a rich mosaic of talented professionals who span different parts of the world.

Cloud Shift develops Agentic AI and blockchain-architected software solutions for clients across cloud and on-premise environments. It also specializes in intelligent workflow automation designs, such as sales leads generation engines and automated market research and analysis platforms.
